BLOODLINE DISPATCH — UNDER THE SKIN
Trailer Voiceover Script v2 — REVISED OPEN
Leads with ticks and boxes. Cal introduced second.
Approx. 210 words / 85 seconds at measured pace
================================================

Farmers are finding boxes.

Plain brown cardboard. No markings. No return address.
Ventilation holes punched in the sides.

And something moving inside.

Hundreds of ticks. Species that have no business being
in the American midwest. Genetically anomalous.
Carrying a modified pathogen that no natural tick
should be able to produce.

Someone put them there.

My name is Cal Morrow. I'm a veterinarian in the Ozark
highlands of Missouri. I spent six years in pharmaceutical
research before I walked away from something I wasn't
supposed to see.

When the animals on my land started dying — I recognized
what I was looking at.

The money trail goes eleven layers deep. A foundation.
A subsidiary. A secret project with forty million dollars
and no public record.

The man funding it has told the world to stop eating
red meat. He owns more American farmland than any
private individual in history.

He is also, deeply, pathologically — terrified of ticks.

I took everything I found to Congress.

He walked out of the courthouse without a single charge.

But biology doesn't care about legal immunity.

And the tick doesn't care who you are.

================================================
DELIVERY NOTES:
- "Farmers are finding boxes." — dead slow. One word at a time almost.
- "And something moving inside." — pause before this line. Let it land.
- "Someone put them there." — flat. Certain. No emotion.
- "He is also, deeply, pathologically — terrified of ticks." — the dash is a full beat pause.
- Final two lines — slowest of all. One breath between each.

KEY CHANGES FROM V1:
- Opens with the boxes/ticks immediately — no introduction first
- Cal's identity revealed AFTER the hook is set
- "Terrified of ticks" line added — the irony that drives the finale
- Shorter overall — tighter for social media retention
